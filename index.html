This interactive map presents an overview of federally declared FEMA disasters at the county level, spanning from 2011 to 2023. Read more on the Atlas of Disaster report by Rebuild by Design.

How To Use

1) Explore Disasters: Simply navigate across the map to view the counties affected by federally declared disasters.

2) Reveal County Information: Click on a county to see the specific number of disasters it has faced and to access the contact information of the corresponding congressional district representative.

3) Take Action: Armed with this information, we encourage you to reach out to your representatives. Share your insights from the map and urge them to prioritize resilient infrastructure projects that safeguard our communities.

Methodology

This map is built on a foundation of research and data sources provided by U.S. Government:

  • FEMA Data: Disasters declared from 2011-2023, sourced from iParametric.
  • Congressional Districts: Boundaries from USDOT BTS shapefiles.
  • Congress Names Database: Representative contact information from United States Github Repository.
  • USA County Shapefiles 2020: Geographic boundaries from ESRI's 2020 shapefiles.
